PID and Lead-Lag Lane-Keeping Controller

Two controllers for a single-track bicycle model at 25 m/s. Routh-Hurwitz analysis showed proportional-only control is conditionally stable and undamped. PID loop-shaping at 12 rad/s reached 66° phase margin and zero steady-state lane error.

A robot that kicks at 200 volts.

I built the kicker electronics for our Small Size League robot: an LT3751 flyback charger that stores energy in a capacitor bank and dumps it through a solenoid to strike the ball. Scroll to take it apart.
